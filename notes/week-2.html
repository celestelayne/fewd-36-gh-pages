Colors

When specifying colors in CSS, you can one of three notations:

  • Hex code. Eg: #FF99AA
  • RGB or RGBA. Eg: rgb(220, 20, 60)
  • Color name. Eg: "black", "blue", "olive", etc.
    Check out all the official names at: Douglas Crockford's color name chart.

Indenting ( and "outdenting")

To indent a bunch of lines all at once, highlight all the lines you want to change, and press the tab key. This moves them to the right one level. To move them back to the left one level, press shift-tab.

Reindent

Easily clean up the indenting for a section of code or an entire file in one fell swoop.
